Vaccines keep us healthy and protect us from catching devastating diseases. As a busy parent, you probably dont have a lot of extra time or may be worried about money. You also try to do everything you can to keep your child healthy.The Nevada Vaccines for Children Program helps provide vaccine to children whose parents or guardians may not be able to afford them. These vaccines protect babies, young children and adolescents from a variety of diseases and is a benefit thats available until your child turns 19.
