The following resources offer easy to understand schedules about immunizations your child will need from 0-18 years old, as well as information on the immunizations that adults need. Infants and Children (birth through 6 years old) English Schedule Spanish Schedule English & Spanish Brochure Pre-teens and Teens Schedule (7 through 18 years old) Tools Make a Schedule for Your Child (for birth through 6 years). For a complete list of recommended immunizations, just select your childs birth date. You can personalize it by adding your child's name. Once printed, review with your child's doctor. Catch-up Scheduler This tool can be used to determine the vaccines your child needs and is especially useful for quickly seeing missed or skipped vaccines according to the ACIP Recommended Immunization Schedule.
