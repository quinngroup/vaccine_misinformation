A Vaccine Information Statement (VIS) is a one-page (two-sided) information sheet produced by CDC. VISs inform vaccine recipients or their parents or legal representatives about the benefits and risks of a vaccine. The law requires that providers of vaccines give out a VIS before certain vaccinations are given.
