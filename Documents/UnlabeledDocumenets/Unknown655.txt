VFC providers must comply with current immunization schedules, dosages and contraindications as established by the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP).The U.S. Recommended Childhood & Adolescent Immunization Schedule indicates the recommended ages for routine administration of currently licensed childhood vaccines for children through age 18 years.All ACIP recommended vaccines for a providers patient population must be kept in supply and made available to eligible patients.Doses not given at the recommended age should be given at any future visit when indicated and feasible.Additional vaccines may be licensed and recommended during the year.
