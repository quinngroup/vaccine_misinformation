The Washington State Childhood Vaccine Program provides vaccine to all kids less than 19 years of age in Washington. The program uses a combination of federal and state funds to purchase vaccines. State and local public health offices help providers enroll in the program, order vaccines, and meet program requirements.For more information contact us at 1-866-397-0337 or WAChildhoodVaccines@doh.wa.gov.For more information about state-supplied vaccine and distribution or storage and handling, and links to our forms, visit the Vaccine Supply and Distribution or Vaccine Storage and Handling page.
