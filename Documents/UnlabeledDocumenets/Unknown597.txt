The New York State Vaccines for Children (NYS VFC) Program is a federally-funded program that provides vaccines at no cost to eligible children. The vaccines are distributed to private physicians' offices and public health clinics enrolled as VFC providers. VFC providers administer the vaccines to VFC-eligible children.
