Vaccine Information Statements (VIS) are information sheets produced by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) that explain both the benefits and the risks of a vaccine. Federal law requires thatthese be handed out to vaccine recipients, their parents, or their legal representatives before certain vaccinations are given.Thesestatements are taken from the CDC. They are the most up-to-date versions of the forms and are in .pdf format for easier viewing and printing.Statements inmore than20 languages can also be downloaded from the Immunization Action Coalitionwebsite.
